About SOA NCC

Established as an integral part of the prestigious 4 (O) CTC and 1 (O) GIRLS BN under the Odisha Directorate, SOA NCC Unit stands as a beacon of excellence in character development, leadership training, and national service.

Affiliated with Siksha 'O' Anusandhan University, we are committed to nurturing young minds into disciplined citizens and future leaders who embody the highest ideals of patriotism, integrity, and service.

Core Principles

NCC Objectives

Building character, developing leadership, and fostering national unity through disciplined training and service

Aim

  • Develop character, camaraderie, discipline, and leadership
  • Shaping organized and trained youth for the future
  • Provide environment that motivates young people

Vision

  • Empower youth volunteers to become leaders
  • Foster leadership and character development
  • Create disciplined and responsible citizens

Motto

  • Uphold Unity and Discipline as core values
  • Promote National Integration and a secular outlook
  • Encourage the spirit of adventure and sportsmanship
Our Activities

Excellence in Action

Engaging in diverse activities that build character, serve the nation, and develop exceptional leadership skills

Experience the precision and discipline of NCC parade platoon. Our cadets demonstrate exceptional coordination through synchronized drill movements and ceremonial displays.

Drill & Discipline

Precision drill and ceremonial parades showcasing discipline and coordination

Take part in trekking, camping, mountaineering, survival skills, and other adventure activities that build courage, endurance, teamwork, and confidence.

Adventure Activities

NCC cadets train in mountaineering, trekking, and survival skills through adventure camps.

Contributing to environmental conservation through extensive tree plantation drives. We're committed to creating a greener future for generations to come.

Extracurricular Activities

Involvement of cadets in Sports, Debate, Cultural, Drone Tech, Public Speaking, etc.

Encouraging innovative thinking and problem-solving. Our cadets actively participate in startup challenges, hackathons, and projects that address real-world needs.

Startup & Innovation

Fostering creativity and entrepreneurial spirit among cadets

Cadets actively participate in national and international-level camps and competitions, engaging in leadership and skill-building activities to gain knowledge, confidence, and experience.

National Camps

Cadets earn national/state representation through national camps like YEP, RDC, TSC, etc.

Our cadets support society through disaster relief, blood donation, cleanliness drives, and awareness campaigns, building empathy and responsibility.

Social Service

Serving communities through health camps, cleanliness drives, and disaster relief.
